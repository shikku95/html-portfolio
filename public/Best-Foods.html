The best Foods according to Shikku

My top 3 Foods of all-time.

1.Chicken Dum Biriyani

Chicken biryani is a delicious Pakistani/Indian rice dish that's typically reserved
for special occasions such as weddings, parties, or holidays such as Ramadan. It
has a lengthy preparation, but the work is definitely worth it. For biryani, basmati
rice is the ideal variety to use.

2.Parotta with Chicken/beef curry

Parotta is a traditional Southern Indian flatbread, usually sold as street food. It is
also popular in Malaysia and Sri Lanka. It is madewith oil or ghee, water, refined
wheat flour known as maida (unlike parathas, which are made with regular wheat),
and occasionally eggs.

3.Chicken Mandhi

The name kuzhimanthi is a portmanteau of mandi and kuzhy, the Malayalam word for
the deep pit in which this dish has to be cooked. Rice in a biryani is often dum, that is,
tightly covered and cooked with steam, and cooked with oil.
